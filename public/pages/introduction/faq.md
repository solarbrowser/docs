# FAQ

Welcome to the Solar FAQ! A curated set of questions insights and gentle guidance designed to help you understand refine and elevate your browsing experience. (nobody asked btw)

## When is Solar coming out?

Solar is planned for the second half of 2026. The timeline may extend into the first half of 2027 as we aim for a fully polished and distinctive experience. We are shaping every part of Solar with care precision and long term vision.


## Why you don't use Chromium or Firefox?

Solar follows a different vision. We are building a browser that understands the web not by inheriting decades of legacy but by reimagining its foundations. Chromium & Firefox are powerful yet it carry a weight that limit the freedom to design a new architecture a new engine philosophy and a new approach to performance and privacy. Solar grows from its own roots giving us the ability to craft behavior structure and optimization without compromise.

## Why choose Solar?

Solar gives you a browser shaped by intention clarity and freedom. A space where speed privacy and control come together without carrying the weight of old architectures. It is built for those who want technology that feels precise thoughtful and light.

### Customization

Solar lets you shape every layer of your browser from behavior to interface giving you a sense of control that feels rare deliberate and empowering.

### Privacy

Your data stays yours. Solar isolates tracking surfaces minimizes exposure and avoids unnecessary background activity creating a quiet space where you are not watched.

### Speed

A modern engine built from clean foundations moves without friction. Loading rendering and interaction feel immediate fluid and effortless.

### Universal extension support

You are not locked into a closed world. Solar supports modern extension standards so your tools follow you freely wherever you go.

### Lightness

The browser stays minimal efficient and calm. No bloat no noise just a focused experience that feels stable and weightless.

## Which platforms does Solar support?

Solar will be available on Windows, macOS, Linux, mobile platforms, and all the platforms you can imagine. We aim to ensure a consistent and fast experience across every device, giving you freedom and flexibility wherever you browse.
